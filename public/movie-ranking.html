AIR AMERICA

The Few, The Proud, The Totally Insane

Mel Gibson and Rovert Downey, Jr. are two renegade pilots ensnared in the madness of covert operations over Laos during the Vietnam conflict.

They lead the crew of AIR AMERICA, a not-so-secret airline that drops everything from live pigs to opium over villages throughout the Vietnam ountryside.

Join Mel, Rovert and the crazy crew of wartime flyers in the funniest action/comedy since Lethal Weapon 2" and "Good Morning, Vietnam."
